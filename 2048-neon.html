How to play

↕

Slide tiles

Use arrow keys, WASD, or swipe to slide all tiles in one direction at once.

+

Merge tiles

When two tiles with the same number collide, they merge into one with their combined value.

⇧

Score points

Every merge adds the tile value to your score. Chain merges for massive point combos.

★

Reach 2048

Build up to the 2048 tile to win. But the game keeps going — how high can you push it?
